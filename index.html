We're just the platform connecting you — we don't own or manage any parking spots.

What You Shouldn't Do

5. Listing Parking Spots

  • Provide accurate information about the spot
  • Include clear photos (up to 5 images)
  • Set fair pricing (₹5–₹100 per hour)
  • Keep your availability updated
  • Honor all confirmed bookings

You can't unlist a spot if there are active bookings — you need to honor those first.

6. Booking and Payments

How Bookings Work

  1. You select dates and time slots
  2. You review the total price
  3. You pay through Razorpay (our payment partner)
  4. Once paid, your booking is confirmed

Commission

  • We charge a 10% commission on each booking
  • The parking spot owner gets 90% of the booking amount
  • You can see all commission details in your transaction history

Payouts

  • Parking spot owners receive payouts to their bank account
  • Payout timing depends on payment processing
  • Make sure your bank details are correct

7. No Cancellations Policy

Important: All bookings are final and non-refundable.

  • Once you confirm a booking, you can't cancel it
  • No refunds for unused or partially used bookings
  • This applies to both parking owners and drivers

We may make exceptions in rare cases (like technical errors), but that's at our discretion.

8. Your Responsibilities

If You're a Parking Spot Owner

  • Make sure you have the right to rent the spot
  • Keep the spot safe and usable
  • Honor all confirmed bookings
  • Provide clear access instructions
  • Make sure the spot is available during booked times

If You're a Driver

  • Use the spot only for parking your vehicle
  • Follow the owner's instructions
  • Don't damage anything
  • Arrive and leave within your booked time slots

Damage

  • Drivers are responsible for any damage they cause

  • We're not responsible for damage at parking spots
