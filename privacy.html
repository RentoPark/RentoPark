Privacy Policy for RentoPark

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Hi! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information when you use RentoPark. We're a small startup, so we keep things simple and transparent. By using RentoPark, you agree to how we handle your information as described here.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

  • Account Info: Email, password (stored securely), name, phone number, and optional profile photo.
  • Banking Info (for parking owners): Bank details for payouts. We don't store card info — Razorpay handles that.
  • Parking Spot Listings: Name, address, location, photos, price, availability, amenities, and driver instructions.
  • Bookings: Dates, times, and payments (via Razorpay).

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device info (phone, app version)
  • Location (if allowed)
  • App usage (to improve performance)
  • Notification tokens (for updates)

Third-Party Services

  • Google (sign-in, maps)
  • Razorpay (payments)
  • Supabase (database & storage)
  • Expo (framework)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Show nearby parking
  • Send booking confirmations
  • Pay parking spot owners (90% of amount)
  • Improve the app and security

4. Who Sees Your Information

  • Other Users: Owners see driver info; drivers see listing details.
  • Service Providers: Razorpay, Supabase, Google, and Expo (all secure).
  • Legal: We may share info if required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • View and update your profile
  • See your bookings and transactions
  • Delete your account anytime
  • Control location and notification permissions
  • Request your data via rentoparkbusiness@gmail.com

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure password storage
  • Razorpay for payments
  • We can't guarantee absolute security but do our best

7. Data Storage & Retention

  • Data stored on Supabase servers
  • Cached locally for faster use
  • Deleted after account removal (except records required by law)

8. Children's Privacy

RentoPark is for users 18+ only. We don't knowingly collect info from minors. Parents can request deletion via email.

9. Commission & Payments

  • 10% commission on bookings
  • 90% payout to owners
  • All payments handled securely via Razorpay

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use means you accept the changes.

11. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email rentoparkbusiness@gmail.com. We’ll respond soon.

12. Indian Law

This policy follows Indian data protection laws.

13. Consent

By using RentoPark, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
